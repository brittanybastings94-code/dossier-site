WHAT'S IN THE DOSSIER

For the planning.

A Pinterest-style outfit board for every occasion. Pin places to eat, drink, do, and stay. File flights and travel into the agenda. See everything on a shared map.

For the conversation.

Every outfit, place, and agenda item has its own reactions and comments. So when someone says "love this," there's no question what they meant.

For the keeping.

The dossier doesn't disappear when the trip ends. Open it next year and remember what you wore, where you ate, and what made everyone laugh.
