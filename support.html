Common questions

How do I sign in?
The Dossier uses a magic link. Enter your email and we'll send you a one-time code to sign in — no password to remember.

How do I invite people to a trip?
Open a trip, tap invite, and share the link. Anyone you invite can see and add to that trip's outfits, places and agenda.

How do I delete my account?
Email hi@dossierapp.co and we'll remove your account and the information associated with it.
